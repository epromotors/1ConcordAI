Our Story

Born in the Cloud,
Built for Resilient Enterprises

As cloud architectures grew increasingly complex, our founders realized that traditional security boundaries were failing. Attackers weren't just exploiting software bugs; they were exploiting identity pathways across decentralized cloud environments.

Orchid Security was created to address this gap. By combining Cloud Security Posture Management (CSPM), Identity Threat Detection, and intelligent risk prioritization, we empower modern security operations to stay ahead of threats.
